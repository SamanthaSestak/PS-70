Week 5: 3D Design & Printing

This week was all about hands-on experience with 3D design and printing, using Fusion to model objects and a 3D printer to bring those designs to life. Alongside learning the process of 3D printing, I experimented with object scanning through photogrammetry...

Part 1: 3D Printing Mjölnir

My goal this week was to create something meaningful for my little sister. My siblings and I are huge Marvel fans, and her favorite character is Thor, so I set out to design and 3D print Mjölnir...

To begin the design process, I used Fusion and followed an online tutorial to develop a basic hammer shape...

Once satisfied with the design, I exported it into PrusaSlicer, a tool that prepares 3D models for printing...

The final product turned out great, although it was smaller than I initially imagined...
