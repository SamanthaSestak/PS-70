Final Project Proposal

Here are my ideas for the final project:

Idea 1: Interactive Smart Mirror

Using sensors and display technology to create a mirror that provides real-time information like weather, reminders, and health stats.

Idea 2: 3D Printed Prosthetic Arm

A prosthetic arm with customizable features, focusing on ease of use, lightweight design, and affordability.

Idea 3: IoT-Enabled Garden

An automated garden system with sensors for moisture, light, and temperature, controlled via a mobile app for optimal plant growth.
